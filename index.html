Urban flow modelling aims not merely at data optimisation — but at balancing the Yin and Yang of urban flux: excessive congestion is "Yin stagnation"; fluid circulation is "Yang flow". SUMO simulation seeks balance of the city's Qi (氣) — achieving collective harmony.

甲骨文
🦴 甲骨文 · 数据预测的起源Oracle Bones · The Origin of Data Prediction

解读现代大数据，需要与商代甲骨文（Jiaguwen）占卜师同样的精准预测精神——从混沌的征兆中提取可预测的规律，将噪声转化为可解释信号，将复杂数据转化为可验证判断。

Reading modern big data requires the same spirit of precise prediction as Shang dynasty oracle bone (Jiaguwen) diviners — extracting predictable patterns from chaotic omens, transforming noise into interpretable signals and complex data into verifiable judgments.

盘古
🌌 盘古开天地 · 数据建模使命Pangu Separates Chaos · Data Modelling Mission

如同盘古以神斧将混沌劈开——轻者升天，重者为地——建模工作的使命是：以数学框架为斧，将大数据的原始混沌分离为可解释的"天"（清晰信号）与可舍弃的"地"（统计噪声）。这正是每次建模工作的目标——为集体判断提供可信的分析地基。

As Pangu split primordial chaos — light upward into Heaven, heavy downward into Earth — the modelling mission is: using mathematical frameworks as the axe, separating big data's raw chaos into interpretable "Heaven" (clear signals) and discardable "Earth" (statistical noise). This is the purpose of each modelling effort — providing a reliable analytical foundation for collective judgment.

围棋
♟ 围棋策略 · Wei Chi 建模哲学Wei Chi Strategy · Modelling Philosophy

变量如围棋黑白子：不是只追求单步最优，而是通过全局布局、边际取舍与长期势能构建稳健的集体决策框架。复杂供应链系统的治理需要"布局"思维，而不是"将军"思维。

Variables are Go stones — black and white. Not optimising single moves, but building robust collective decision frameworks through global positioning, marginal trade-offs, and long-term potential. Complex supply chain systems require "positional" thinking, not "checkmate" thinking.
